E-library

An electronic library, often referred to as an e-library, is a digital platform that offers access to a vast collection of resources such as books, journals, articles, magazines, research papers, and other types of documents. Unlike traditional libraries that house physical copies of materials, e-libraries provide users with the convenience of accessing information electronically from the comfort of their own devices.
